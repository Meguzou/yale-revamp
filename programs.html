Our Programs

Discover your artistic path at Yale School of Art

MFA in Graphic Design

Duration: 2 years

Our MFA program in Graphic Design focuses on the development of a cohesive, investigative body of work, also known as the student's thesis. Students are expected to have a command of contemporary critical discourse.

  • Typography and Type Design
  • Interactive Design
  • Motion Graphics
  • Design Theory and History

MFA in Painting/Printmaking

Duration: 2 years

The Painting/Printmaking program offers a unique combination of studio work, critical studies, and exposure to various media and approaches to visual art.

  • Oil and Acrylic Painting
  • Traditional and Digital Printmaking
  • Mixed Media
  • Contemporary Art Theory

MFA in Photography

Duration: 2 years

Our photography program encourages the exploration of lens-based image-making through both traditional and contemporary approaches.

  • Digital Photography
  • Darkroom Techniques
  • Video and Moving Image
  • Photo History and Theory

MFA in Sculpture

Duration: 2 years

The Sculpture program provides a foundation in contemporary sculptural practices, emphasizing spatial awareness, material transformation, and critical thinking.

  • 3D Design and Fabrication
  • Installation Art
  • Digital Sculpture
  • Public Art
